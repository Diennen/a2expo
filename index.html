About us

Our dynamically growing company, headquartered in Wroclaw (Poland), specializes in high-quality services related to the assembly and disassembly of stands, as well as the production and efficient transportation of materials.

We delivering our professional services across the territories of the European Union, the United Kingdom, and the United States, aiming to provide customers with an outstanding level of service and satisfaction.

Our services

Stand construction

Individual solutions for crafting unique exhibition spaces. We specialize in providing comprehensive solutions that ensure a seamless and memorable exhibition experience.

Stand dismantling

Streamlining your event closure process. Our dedicated team ensures the swift and meticulous disassembly of stands, allowing you to wrap up your event seamlessly, without any hassle. Experience hassle-free teardowns with our expert dismantling services.

Material crafting

Provision of individual solutions, starting from creative concepts and ending with experienced implementation. Trust us in creating outstanding materials for stands that will not only distinguish your brand but also capture the attention of your audience.

Material transport

We ensure efficient and reliable material transportation, delivering professional solutions for your business.

3D stand mockups

Bringing your future event to life. We craft realistic 3D models, offering you the opportunity to visualize every detail of your upcoming stand. Trust us to create a virtual space that will showcase your brand.

We operate

Our company provides services both on the national market and on the global market. We operate successfully in the territories of the European Union, the United States, and the United Kingdom, ensuring high-quality service across an extensive area.
